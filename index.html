Family

Our may be first and foremost community and foundation is Family. We start out pure and innocent towards the world, in arms' length and warmth protected by them. Yet there will be moments where they may upset you, hurt you, and much more. But that doesn't require the title for Family. That circle may come late, and may come early luckily enough, but enough reason to hold on. Family is not supposed to be heavy, it is supposed to be the easening of the burdens or at least the people who will sit with you through it. Family is not the water that gets into your boat to sink it, it is the people who will help you patch yourself up as you steer your way towards your lighthouse.

Social Life/Friends

As we get older, we interacted with strangers, who are frequently became our friends. They refer to a group of pals as "sisters-by-heart" or even "soul-buddies." Growing connections is necessary for friendship to flourish. Experiences with them assist to develop a solid bond in which they are prepared to stick with you through thick and thin. They can provide us their shoulders if we need someone to weep on, act as our guides if we feel lost on the journey, help us celebrate the good moments when the going gets tough, or indeed listen to our private thoughts and secrets. Even if they made a mistake or witnessed them make a poor decision, we often feel envious, but speaking out and calling them out might be useful. Additionally, you must repay the favors they will perform for you if you want them to be around longer. Having an emotional support or accountability partner is important. It's crucial that you have a partner who will encourage your growth, keep you open to change, and help you manage your life more efficiently so that you may live it fully.
